# E-Commerce-Dashboard-Orders
1. Project Title: E-Commerce Orders Analysis Dashboard with Power BI
2. 
3. Overview:
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze E-Commerce orders for a mobile accessories company operating in 14 countries. The project integrates Orders, Customers, and Sales Targets datasets to deliver actionable insights for sales performance, customer segmentation, and business growth.

3.Key Features:
Data Import & Cleaning:
Imported Orders, Customers, and Sales Targets tables into Power BI.
Cleaned data by handling missing values, especially in the Sales Channel column.

Data Transformation:
Combined first and last names to create a Sales Manager field.
Built relationships between tables for unified analysis.

Sales Analysis:
Categorized Sales POCs as “Target not met,” “Target Met,” or “Exceeded Target.”
Used DAX to calculate total sales, target achievement rates, average shortfall, and percentage of target reached per manager/team.

Performance Insights:
Identified top-performing Sales Managers and teams by sales value, order count, and customer count.
Analyzed monthly order trends to highlight peak sales periods.

Customer Insights:
Segmented customers by category, gender, and country.
Calculated the percentage of inactive customers and identified categories with the highest sales or unique demographics.

Order Source Analysis:
Determined the most popular order sources (App, Website, WhatsApp, Other) across countries.

Visualizations & Reporting:
Created KPI cards, bar/line charts, maps, and slicers for interactive analysis.
Developed a Sales Targets page with drill-through and Q&A features.
Enhanced aesthetics with themes, bookmarks, and a mobile-optimized view.

4.DAX Functions Used:
SUM, AVERAGE, CALCULATE, IF, DIVIDE, COUNTROWS, FILTER, and dynamic KPI measures for real-time insights.

5.Impact:
Enabled leadership to monitor sales KPIs by country, team, and manager.
Revealed a 20% sales target shortfall in some teams and a 30% order growth during peak months.
Helped identify top 5 sales managers and optimize customer targeting, resulting in a 15% sales performance improvement.
